Research focus area

My research is focused on the role of vestibular cues for visual perception and the interaction between self-motion and the visual perception of object motion in our environment.

I have furthermore a strong interest in Philosophy of Science and I am a defender of Open Science practices, as well as a proud member and supporter of the LGBTIQA2S+ community.
